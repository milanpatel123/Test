Our Introduction

The Gargee Seva Foundation, Pune, has started the organization for social work with the intention of giving something to the community in which you live. Vijaykumar Gawde is the president of this organization, Archana Mohite as the vice president and Suhasini Gawde is the secretary. The aim and objectives of this organization have been kept in a good manner, introduction of educational programs in it, organizing competitions such as distribution of books to poorer students, vocal competition, drama, art and painting,

Our Work

Various activities like organizing sports competitions, library and reading room will be organized by this organization. In addition to this, health facilities like blood donation, ophthalmology, primary health check-up, as well as many commendable activities have been planned for the poor in the society. We are open for the annual subscription Membership. Gargi Services Foundation will always take the initiative to help the poor.
